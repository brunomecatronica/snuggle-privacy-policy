2.2 Information Collected Automatically

2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Technical support: Resolve issues and improve service
  • Security: Prevent misuse and protect data

Legal Basis (LGPD/GDPR)

  • Consent: For voluntarily provided data

4. STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Where We Store

  • Firebase (Google Cloud - us-central1)
  • Local device: Temporary cache and settings
  • Google Play Services: Authentication data

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit: HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: AES-256
  • Secure authentication: OAuth 2.0 with Google
  • Restricted access: Authorized personnel only
  • Automated backups
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active data: While the account is active
  • Deleted data: Removed within 30 days
  • Security logs: Retained for 90 days
  • Crash data: Anonymized after 180 days

5. DATA SHARING

5.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We NEVER sell, rent, or share your data with:

5.2 Limited Sharing
